What is the Keto Diet?

The keto diet, also known as the ketogenic diet, is a low-carb, high-fat diet that has been shown to be effective in helping people lose weight and improve their overall health. The diet works by putting your body in a state of ketosis, which means that it is burning fat for energy instead of carbohydrates.

How Does the Keto Diet Work?

The keto diet works by drastically reducing your carbohydrate intake and increasing your fat intake. When you eat a lot of carbohydrates, your body produces insulin, which helps your cells absorb glucose for energy. However, when you eat a lot of fat and very few carbohydrates, your body produces ketones, which are used for energy instead of glucose.

The Benefits of the Keto Diet

There are several benefits to following a keto diet, including weight loss, improved blood sugar control, increased energy levels, and improved mental clarity. In addition, some studies have shown that the diet may also help reduce the risk of certain diseases, such as cancer and Alzheimer's.
