Welcome to the ultimate guide for finding fun family activities to do on a rainy day in Sydney. Rainy days can be a bummer, but they don't have to be. With our list of indoor activities, you can still have a great time with your family while staying dry. Below is a table of content for easy navigation.

Visit Museums

Sydney has an abundance of museums that cater to both children and adults. The Australian Museum, located in the heart of the city, has a range of exhibitions that showcase Australia's natural history and cultural heritage. The Powerhouse Museum, located in Ultimo, has a range of interactive exhibitions that will keep children entertained for hours. Other museums worth visiting include the Museum of Contemporary Art, the Maritime Museum, and the Art Gallery of New South Wales.

Explore the Aquarium

The SEA LIFE Sydney Aquarium is a great indoor activity for the whole family. Located in Darling Harbour, this aquarium has a range of exhibits that showcase Australia's marine life. From sharks and stingrays to sea turtles and tropical fish, there is something for everyone to see. The aquarium also has a range of interactive experiences, including a glass-bottom boat ride and a penguin encounter.

Watch a Movie

Watching a movie is a classic rainy day activity. Sydney has a range of cinemas that cater to families, including Event Cinemas, Hoyts, and Palace Cinemas. You can catch the latest blockbuster or choose a family-friendly movie that everyone will enjoy. Don't forget to grab some popcorn!

Go Shopping

If you're looking for a way to stay dry and get some shopping done, head to one of Sydney's many shopping centers. Westfield Sydney, located in the heart of the city, has a range of stores that cater to both adults and children. Other shopping centers worth visiting include Broadway Shopping Centre, Bondi Junction, and Chatswood Chase.

Play at the Arcades

If you're looking for a fun activity that's out of the rain, head to an arcade. Timezone, located in the heart of the city, has a range of games that cater to both children and adults. Other arcades worth visiting include Playtime, iPlay, and Zone Bowling.

Create Art Together

Get creative on a rainy day by creating art with your family. You can head to one of Sydney's art supply stores, such as Eckersley's or The Art Scene, and pick up some supplies. Then, find a cozy spot at home and let your creativity flow. You can draw, paint, or make crafts together.

Play Board Games

Board games are a great way to pass the time on a rainy day. Sydney has a range of board game cafes, including The Games Cube and Good Games, where you can play a range of games with your family. Alternatively, you can bring out your own board games and have a family game night at home.

Bake Together

Baking is a fun and tasty way to spend a rainy day with your family. You can choose a recipe that everyone will enjoy, such as cookies or cupcakes, and work together to create something delicious. Don't forget to lick the spoon!

Visit the Library

The library is a great indoor activity that's both educational and fun. Sydney has a range of libraries that cater to families, including the State Library of New South Wales and the City of Sydney Library. You can borrow books, attend storytime sessions, or even participate in a book club.

Visit an Indoor Park

If you're looking for a way to burn off some energy on a rainy day, head to an indoor park. The Monkey Mania chain has multiple locations throughout Sydney and offers a range of indoor play areas that cater to children of all ages. Other indoor parks worth visiting include Lollipops Playland and Inflatable World.

Tips for a Great Day Out

  • Check the weather forecast before heading out to ensure you're prepared for the rain.
  • Bring an umbrella and rain jacket, just in case.
  • Plan ahead and book tickets or make reservations to avoid disappointment.
  • Bring snacks and drinks to keep everyone fueled throughout the day.
  • Don't forget to have fun and make memories with your family!

Conclusion

Rainy days don't have to be boring. With our list of family things to do in Sydney on a rainy day, you can still have a great time with your family while staying dry. From museums and aquariums to board games and baking, there's something for everyone to enjoy. So, grab your umbrella and head out for a fun-filled day with your loved ones!
