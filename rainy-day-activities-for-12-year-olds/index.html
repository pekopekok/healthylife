Rainy Day Activities For 12 Year Olds

When the rain is pouring down outside, it can be difficult to keep your 12 year old entertained. But don't worry, we've got you covered with plenty of fun and exciting activities to keep them busy. From playing board games to creating art projects, there are plenty of options to choose from. So, let's dive into some of the best rainy day activities for 12 year olds.

Table of Contents

  • Board Games
  • Movie Marathon
  • Baking
  • Art Projects
  • Indoor Sports

Board Games

Board games are a classic rainy day activity that never gets old. They're a great way to spend quality time with your child while also having fun. There are so many different types of board games to choose from, so pick one that your child will enjoy. Some popular options include Monopoly, Scrabble, and Clue. You can even make it more exciting by adding prizes for the winner.

How to:

First, choose a board game that you and your child will enjoy. Set up the game and go over the rules. Take turns playing the game, and make sure to have fun!

Tips:

  • Choose a game that is appropriate for your child's age and skill level.
  • Make sure to have snacks and drinks on hand.
  • Consider adding a time limit to the game to keep things moving.

Pros and Cons:

Pros: Board games are a fun way to spend time with your child and can help develop their critical thinking and problem solving skills.

Cons: Some board games can be long and tedious, which may lead to boredom.

Movie Marathon

A movie marathon is a great way to spend a rainy day. Pick a few of your child's favorite movies, make some popcorn, and settle in for a cozy day on the couch. You can even make it more exciting by creating a themed movie marathon, such as a Harry Potter marathon or a Disney marathon.

How to:

First, choose the movies you want to watch. Set up a comfortable viewing area with blankets and pillows. Make some popcorn or other snacks. Start the first movie and enjoy!

Tips:

  • Choose movies that are appropriate for your child's age.
  • Create a cozy atmosphere with blankets and pillows.
  • Consider making themed snacks or decorations to go along with the movie marathon.

Pros and Cons:

Pros: A movie marathon is a relaxing way to spend a rainy day and can be a great way to bond with your child.

Cons: Sitting for long periods of time can be unhealthy, so make sure to take breaks and stretch your legs.

Baking

Baking is a fun and educational rainy day activity for 12 year olds. Not only do they get to enjoy a sweet treat at the end, but they also learn valuable skills such as measuring, following directions, and patience. Choose a recipe that your child will enjoy, such as cookies, brownies, or cupcakes.

How to:

First, choose a recipe and gather all the ingredients. Read through the recipe together and go over any questions your child may have. Follow the recipe step-by-step, making sure to measure everything accurately. Enjoy your delicious treat when it's done!

Tips:

  • Choose a recipe that is appropriate for your child's skill level.
  • Make sure to supervise your child while they are using the oven or stove.
  • Consider making extra to share with friends and family.

Pros and Cons:

Pros: Baking is a fun and educational activity that can result in a delicious treat at the end.

Cons: Baking can be messy and time-consuming, so make sure to set aside enough time and clean up as you go.

Art Projects

Art projects are a great way for 12 year olds to express their creativity and imagination. Plus, it's a great way to keep them busy on a rainy day. Choose a project that your child will enjoy, such as painting, drawing, or making a collage.

How to:

First, gather all the necessary supplies for the project. Set up a workspace and let your child's imagination run wild. Encourage them to be creative and have fun. Display their finished project for all to see!

Tips:

  • Choose a project that is appropriate for your child's skill level.
  • Provide plenty of materials for your child to work with.
  • Encourage your child to be creative and have fun.

Pros and Cons:

Pros: Art projects are a great way for 12 year olds to express their creativity and imagination.

Cons: Art projects can be messy, so make sure to set up a workspace that is easy to clean up.

Indoor Sports

Just because it's raining outside doesn't mean your child can't be active. There are plenty of indoor sports and activities to keep them moving and having fun. Choose an activity that your child will enjoy, such as basketball, soccer, or yoga.

How to:

First, choose an activity that is appropriate for your child's age and skill level. Set up a safe and comfortable space to play. Encourage your child to have fun and stay active.

Tips:

  • Choose an activity that your child is interested in.
  • Make sure the space is safe and free of obstacles.
  • Consider playing with your child to make it more fun and exciting.

Pros and Cons:

Pros: Indoor sports and activities are a great way to keep your child active and healthy, even on a rainy day.

Cons: Some indoor sports and activities may require special equipment or a lot of space, so make sure to plan ahead.

There you have it, our top picks for rainy day activities for 12 year olds. Whether your child enjoys board games, movies, baking, art, or sports, there's something for everyone. So, the next time it's pouring down outside, don't worry - just pick one of these fun and exciting activities and enjoy the day inside!
