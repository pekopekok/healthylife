As Mother's Day approaches, it's time to start thinking about how to show your appreciation and love for the most important woman in your life. One way to do this is by sharing some heartfelt quotes that express your feelings. Here are some of the best Mother's Day quotes in English to inspire you:

Quote 1: "A mother is a person who seeing there are only four pieces of pie for five people, promptly announces she never did care for pie." - Tenneva Jordan

This quote by Tenneva Jordan perfectly captures the selflessness and sacrifice that mothers make for their children. It's a reminder that a mother's love is unconditional and knows no bounds.

Quote 2: "Motherhood is the biggest gamble in the world. It is the glorious life force. It's huge and scary - it's an act of infinite optimism." - Gilda Radner

Gilda Radner's quote highlights the bravery and courage of mothers. Being a mother is a huge responsibility and can be scary at times, but it's also a source of great joy and fulfillment.

Quote 3: "God could not be everywhere, and therefore he made mothers." - Rudyard Kipling

This quote by Rudyard Kipling is a beautiful tribute to the role that mothers play in our lives. It's a reminder that mothers are our protectors, our nurturers, and our guides.

Quote 4: "The heart of a mother is a deep abyss at the bottom of which you will always find forgiveness." - Honore de Balzac

Honore de Balzac's quote speaks to the infinite capacity for love and forgiveness that mothers possess. No matter what mistakes we make, our mothers are always there to offer us their unconditional love and support.

Quote 5: "A mother's arms are more comforting than anyone else's." - Princess Diana

This quote by Princess Diana captures the warmth and comfort that a mother's embrace provides. It's a reminder that no matter how big we get, we always need our mother's love and affection.

Quote 6: "All that I am, or hope to be, I owe to my angel mother." - Abraham Lincoln

This quote by Abraham Lincoln is a beautiful tribute to the impact that a mother can have on her child's life. It's a reminder that our mothers are our biggest supporters and our greatest role models.

Quote 7: "A mother's love for her child is like nothing else in the world. It knows no law, no pity, it dares all things and crushes down remorselessly all that stands in its path." - Agatha Christie

Agatha Christie's quote speaks to the fierce and unyielding love that mothers have for their children. It's a reminder that a mother's love can conquer all obstacles and overcome all challenges.

Quote 8: "Mothers hold their children's hands for a short while, but their hearts forever." - Unknown

This quote by an unknown author captures the bittersweet nature of motherhood. It's a reminder that our mothers may not always be physically present, but their love and guidance will always be with us.

Quote 9: "The best way to make your children good is to make them happy." - Oscar Wilde

Oscar Wilde's quote is a reminder that a mother's role is not just to discipline and teach her children, but also to bring them joy and happiness. It's a reminder that a happy child is a well-adjusted and well-rounded child.

Quote 10: "Being a mother is learning about strengths you didn't know you had, and dealing with fears you didn't know existed." - Linda Wooten

Linda Wooten's quote speaks to the personal growth and self-discovery that comes with motherhood. It's a reminder that being a mother is a journey of self-discovery and personal transformation.

Conclusion

These are just a few of the many beautiful quotes that celebrate the love and sacrifice of mothers. Whether you choose to share these quotes with your mother or simply reflect on them yourself, they are a reminder of the importance and value of motherhood.
