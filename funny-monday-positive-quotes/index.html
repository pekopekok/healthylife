Welcome to 2023, the year where Mondays are no longer dreaded! Whether you're starting a new job or simply trying to make the most of your week, starting your Monday with a positive attitude can set the tone for the entire week. In this post, we will be sharing some funny Monday positive quotes to put a smile on your face and kickstart your week with some good vibes. So, let's dive in!

Quote 1:

"Monday is a fresh start. It's never too late to dig in and begin a new journey of success."

Starting your week on a positive note can help you stay motivated and focused on your goals. Use Monday as a fresh start to begin your journey towards success.


Quote 2:

"Mondays are the start of the workweek which offer new beginnings 52 times a year!"

Instead of viewing Mondays as a drag, think of it as a new opportunity to start fresh and tackle new challenges.


Quote 3:

"Monday is the perfect day to correct last week's mistakes."

Don't let last week's mistakes hold you back. Use Monday to correct them and move forward.


Quote 4:

"Mondays are a lot like getting fat. They make you feel sad, sometimes angry and there is not much scope for liking either fat or Mondays for any reason."

Although Mondays can be tough, try to find humor in the situation. Laughing at the situation can help uplift your mood and start your week on a positive note.


Quote 5:

"Monday, Monday, so good to me; Monday morning, it was all I hoped it would be."

Find joy in the small things on Mondays, like your favorite cup of coffee or a beautiful sunrise. These small moments can make your day much brighter.


Quote 6:

"Monday is like a math problem. Add the irritation, subtract the sleep, multiply the problems, divide the happiness."

Although Mondays can be challenging, remember that they are only one day out of the week. Don't let the challenges of one day overshadow the happiness of the rest of the week.


Quote 7:

"Monday is a state of mind. Put on your positive pants and get stuff done."

Attitude is everything when it comes to Mondays. Reframe your thoughts and approach the day with a positive mindset.


Quote 8:

"Monday, the beginning of a new week. I embrace it, look forward to it, and use it to recharge my batteries."

Use Monday to set the tone for the rest of the week. Take the time to recharge and refresh yourself so you can tackle the week ahead with vigor.


Quote 9:

"If each day is a gift, I'd like to know where to return Monday."

Although Mondays can be tough, it's important to remember that each day is a gift. Find joy in the small moments and make the most of each day.


Quote 10:

"Monday, Monday, can't trust that day."

You may not be able to trust Monday, but you can trust yourself to make the most of the day. Take charge and make Monday work for you.


Conclusion

Starting your week with a positive attitude can set the tone for the entire week. We hope these funny Monday positive quotes put a smile on your face and help you approach Mondays with a newfound sense of motivation and positivity. Remember to approach each day with an open mind and a positive attitude, and you'll be well on your way to success.
