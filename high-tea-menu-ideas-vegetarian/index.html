How to Plan a Vegetarian High Tea Menu

Planning a vegetarian high tea menu can be a bit challenging, especially if you're used to serving traditional high tea dishes that often include meat. However, with a bit of creativity, you can come up with a menu that's just as delicious and elegant.

The first thing you need to do is decide on the number of courses you want to serve. Traditional high tea usually consists of three courses: sandwiches, scones, and sweets. However, you can add more courses if you want to make your event extra special.

Next, you need to choose the dishes you want to serve for each course. For the sandwiches course, you can choose from a variety of vegetarian fillings such as cucumber and cream cheese, egg salad, or hummus and roasted vegetables. For the scones course, you can serve plain or fruit scones with clotted cream and jam. Finally, for the sweets course, you can serve a variety of desserts such as mini cakes, macarons, or petit fours.

Don't forget to offer a variety of teas to your guests, including black, green, and herbal teas. You can also serve coffee or hot chocolate for those who prefer something different.

High Tea Menu Ideas Vegetarian

Here are some high tea menu ideas that are perfect for vegetarians:

Sandwiches Course

  • Cucumber and cream cheese sandwiches
  • Egg salad sandwiches
  • Hummus and roasted vegetable sandwiches
  • Tomato and mozzarella sandwiches
  • Avocado and tomato sandwiches

Scones Course

  • Plain scones with clotted cream and jam
  • Fruit scones with clotted cream and jam

Sweets Course

  • Mini cakes
  • Macarons
  • Petit fours
  • Fruit tarts
  • Chocolate truffles

Tips for Hosting a Vegetarian High Tea

Here are some tips for hosting a successful vegetarian high tea:

  • Offer a variety of vegetarian options for each course
  • Make sure to label all the dishes so your guests know what they're eating
  • Offer a selection of teas, including black, green, and herbal teas
  • Don't forget to provide coffee and hot chocolate for those who prefer something different
  • Decorate your table with fresh flowers and elegant china
  • Provide comfortable seating for your guests
  • Play soft background music to create a relaxing atmosphere

Frequently Asked Questions

What is high tea?

High tea is a British tradition that typically consists of tea, sandwiches, scones, and sweets. It's usually served in the late afternoon or early evening.

Can you have a vegetarian high tea?

Yes, it's possible to have a vegetarian high tea. You can substitute meat-based dishes with vegetarian options, such as hummus and roasted vegetable sandwiches.

What should I serve at a vegetarian high tea?

You can serve a variety of vegetarian dishes such as cucumber and cream cheese sandwiches, fruit scones with clotted cream and jam, and mini cakes.

Pros and Cons of Hosting a Vegetarian High Tea

Pros

  • Vegetarian high tea is a great way to cater to guests who are vegetarian or have dietary restrictions
  • It's an opportunity to get creative with vegetarian dishes
  • Vegetarian high tea can be just as delicious and elegant as traditional high tea

Cons

  • It can be challenging to come up with vegetarian dishes that are suitable for high tea
  • Some guests may prefer meat-based dishes

Overall, hosting a vegetarian high tea is a great way to cater to guests who have dietary restrictions while still serving delicious and elegant dishes. With a bit of creativity, you can come up with a menu that will impress your guests and make your event extra special.
