Things To Do In Daytona Beach Florida For Families

Written by Alvine Jul 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

.

If you are planning a family vacation in Daytona Beach, Florida, you will find plenty of activities to keep everyone entertained. From the beaches to the amusement parks, there is something for everyone. In this article, we will explore the top things to do in Daytona Beach, Florida for families.

Table of Contents

  • Beaches
  • Amusement Parks
  • Museums
  • Water Activities
  • Food and Restaurants
  • Shopping

Beaches

Daytona Beach is known for its beautiful beaches. With miles of sandy shoreline, families can relax in the sun, swim, and build sandcastles. Here are some of the top beaches to visit:

Daytona Beach

Daytona Beach is the most popular beach in the area. It offers plenty of parking, restrooms, and showers. Families can rent beach chairs and umbrellas or bring their own. There are also plenty of restaurants and shops nearby.

New Smyrna Beach

New Smyrna Beach is located just south of Daytona Beach. It is a quieter beach with fewer crowds. Families can swim, surf, and fish. There are also several parks and playgrounds nearby.

Amusement Parks

Daytona Beach is home to several amusement parks that are perfect for families. Here are some of the top parks to visit:

Daytona Lagoon

Daytona Lagoon is a water park and family entertainment center. It features water slides, a lazy river, a wave pool, and mini-golf. There are also several restaurants and an arcade.

Speedway Go-Karts

Speedway Go-Karts is a family-friendly go-kart track. It offers single and double karts, as well as kiddie karts. There is also an arcade and mini-golf.

Museums

Daytona Beach has several museums that are perfect for families. Here are some of the top museums to visit:

MOAS - Museum of Arts and Sciences

MOAS is a museum that features art, science, and history exhibits. It has a planetarium, a children's museum, and a sculpture garden.

Marine Science Center

The Marine Science Center is a museum that focuses on marine life. Families can learn about sea turtles, fish, and other sea creatures. There is also a touch tank and outdoor exhibits.

Water Activities

In addition to the beaches and water parks, Daytona Beach offers several water activities that families can enjoy. Here are some of the top water activities:

Dolphin and Manatee Tours

Families can take a boat tour to see dolphins and manatees in the wild. There are several tour companies that offer this activity.

Paddleboarding

Families can rent paddleboards and explore the waterways in Daytona Beach. There are several rental companies that offer this activity.

Food and Restaurants

Daytona Beach has several restaurants that are perfect for families. Here are some of the top restaurants to visit:

Crabby Joe's Deck and Grill

Crabby Joe's is a beachside restaurant that offers seafood, burgers, and sandwiches. Families can dine on the deck and enjoy the ocean view.

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co.

Bubba Gump Shrimp Co. is a seafood restaurant that is themed after the movie Forrest Gump. Families can enjoy shrimp, fish, and other seafood dishes.

Shopping

Daytona Beach has several shopping areas that families can visit. Here are some of the top shopping areas:

Volusia Mall

Volusia Mall is a shopping center that features over 120 stores. Families can shop for clothing, electronics, and other items.

Ocean Walk Shoppes

Ocean Walk Shoppes is a shopping and entertainment complex. It features several restaurants, a movie theater, and shops. In conclusion, Daytona Beach, Florida offers plenty of activities for families. From the beaches to the amusement parks, there is something for everyone. Plan your next family vacation to Daytona Beach and enjoy all that this beautiful city has to offer.
