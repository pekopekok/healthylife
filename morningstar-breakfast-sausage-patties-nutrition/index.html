What are Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties?

Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties are a vegetarian alternative to traditional sausage patties. They are made from a blend of soy protein, wheat gluten, and natural flavors to create a tasty and protein-packed breakfast option.

How to Prepare Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties?

Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties can be cooked in a variety of ways, including in the oven, on the stovetop, or in the microwave. Follow the package instructions for best results.

Stovetop Instructions:

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the frozen patties and cook for 6-7 minutes, flipping occasionally, until heated through and browned.
  3. Remove from heat and serve.

Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties Nutrition Facts

One Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patty contains:

  • Calories: 80
  • Protein: 9g
  • Fat: 4g (0.5g saturated)
  • Carbohydrates: 3g
  • Sodium: 240mg

They are also a good source of iron, providing 10% of your daily recommended intake in one patty.

Benefits of Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties

There are several benefits to choosing Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties as part of your breakfast routine.

High in Protein

With 9g of protein per patty, Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties are a great way to start your day with sustainable energy. Protein is essential for building and repairing muscles, and it can also help you feel fuller for longer, reducing the likelihood of snacking between meals.

Vegetarian and Vegan-Friendly

If you follow a vegetarian or vegan diet, Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties are a great way to get your protein fix without compromising your beliefs. They are made with plant-based ingredients and are certified vegan by the Vegan Society.

Low in Calories

At only 80 calories per patty, Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties are a great option for those looking to maintain or lose weight. They are also low in fat, with only 4g per patty.

Potential Drawbacks of Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties

While Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties are a great option for many people, there are some potential drawbacks to keep in mind.

Processed Ingredients

While Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties are made with plant-based ingredients, they are highly processed. This means they may contain additives or preservatives that some people prefer to avoid.

High in Sodium

One Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patty contains 240mg of sodium, which is 10% of your daily recommended intake. If you are trying to limit your sodium intake, Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties may not be the best option for you.

FAQs

Are Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties Healthy?

Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties can be a healthy addition to your breakfast routine, providing a good source of protein and iron. However, they are highly processed and contain a moderate amount of sodium, so they should be consumed in moderation as part of a balanced diet.

Can You Freeze Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties?

Yes, Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties can be frozen for up to six months. To thaw, simply leave them in the refrigerator overnight or heat them in the microwave.

Conclusion

Morningstar Breakfast Sausage Patties are a tasty and convenient way to start your day with a protein-packed breakfast. While they are highly processed and contain a moderate amount of sodium, they can be a healthy addition to your diet when consumed in moderation.
