How Many Calories Per Day for Exercise?

The number of calories required per day for exercise depends on various factors such as age, gender, weight, height, and activity level. Generally, an average person should consume around 2000-2500 calories per day. However, if you want to lose weight, then you need to burn more calories than you consume.

A pound of fat is equal to 3500 calories. Therefore, to lose one pound of fat per week, you need to burn around 500 calories per day. If you want to lose more weight, then you need to burn more calories per day.

How to Calculate the Number of Calories Required Per Day for Exercise?

To calculate the number of calories required per day for exercise, you need to determine your basal metabolic rate (BMR). BMR is the number of calories your body requires to function at rest. Once you know your BMR, you can multiply it by an activity factor to determine the number of calories required per day for exercise.

Step-By-Step Guide to Calculate the Number of Calories Required Per Day for Exercise

Step 1: Calculate your BMR using the Harris-Benedict equation.

Step 2: Determine your activity level using the following chart:

Activity LevelDescriptionActivity Factor
SedentaryLittle or no exercise1.2
Lightly ActiveLight exercise or sports 1-3 days per week1.375
Moderately ActiveModerate exercise or sports 3-5 days per week1.55
Very ActiveHard exercise or sports 6-7 days per week1.725
Extra ActiveVery hard exercise or sports, physical job or training twice per day1.9

Step 3: Multiply your BMR by the activity factor to determine the number of calories required per day for exercise.

Tips to Burn More Calories During Exercise

Here are some tips to burn more calories during exercise:

  • Increase the intensity of your workout
  • Incorporate strength-training exercises
  • Do high-intensity interval training (HIIT)
  • Increase the duration of your workout
  • Incorporate cardio exercises such as cycling, running, and swimming

Solution to Burn More Calories

The solution to burn more calories during exercise is to make exercise a part of your daily routine. Try to incorporate different types of exercises in your routine and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workout.

FAQs

Q. How many calories should I burn per day for weight loss?

A. To lose one pound of fat per week, you need to burn around 500 calories per day. However, the number of calories required per day for weight loss depends on various factors such as age, gender, weight, height, and activity level.

Q. Can I lose weight by exercise alone?

A. No, exercise alone cannot lead to weight loss. You also need to maintain a healthy and balanced diet to lose weight.

Pros and Cons of Burning Calories During Exercise

Pros:

  • Burning calories during exercise helps in weight loss
  • Exercise improves overall health and fitness
  • Exercise reduces the risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes and heart disease

Cons:

  • Exercise can be time-consuming and require dedication
  • Over-exercising can lead to injuries and burnout
  • Exercising alone cannot lead to weight loss, a balanced diet is also required

In conclusion, it is important to understand how many calories per day for exercise are required to lose weight. By following the above-mentioned tips and solutions, you can burn more calories during exercise and achieve your weight loss goals.
