What is High Tea?

High tea, also known as afternoon tea, is a British tradition that originated in the 19th century. It is a formal tea service that typically includes a variety of tea sandwiches, scones with clotted cream and jam, cakes, and pastries. High tea is usually served between 3 pm and 5 pm and is often enjoyed by groups of friends or family members.

How Did High Tea Originate?

The origin of high tea can be traced back to the Industrial Revolution in England. During this time, the working class would return home from work around 6 pm and have a meal that consisted of bread, butter, cheese, and tea. This meal was known as "high tea" because it was served at a high table or dining table. The upper class, on the other hand, would have a lighter meal in the afternoon, which included tea and small cakes or sandwiches. This meal was known as "low tea" because it was served on a low table or coffee table. Over time, the two meals became combined, and high tea became a more formal affair.

What Can You Expect from a High Tea Experience?

When you attend a high tea, you can expect to be served a variety of tea sandwiches, scones with clotted cream and jam, cakes, and pastries. The tea is usually served in a teapot, and you can choose from a variety of teas. The atmosphere is usually formal, with tablecloths, fine china, and silverware. It is a lovely way to spend an afternoon with friends or family members.

How to Prepare for a High Tea?

If you are planning to attend a high tea, there are a few things you can do to prepare. First, make sure you dress appropriately. High tea is a formal affair, so dress in your best attire. Second, make a reservation in advance, as high tea is a popular event and can fill up quickly. Finally, be sure to arrive on time, as high tea is served promptly at 3 pm or 4 pm.

FAQs About High Tea:

What is the Difference Between Low Tea and High Tea?

Low tea is a lighter meal that is usually served in the afternoon and includes tea and small cakes or sandwiches. High tea is a more formal affair that is served in the early evening and includes heartier fare such as scones, tea sandwiches, cakes, and pastries.

Do I Need to Dress Up for High Tea?

Yes, high tea is a formal affair, so it is best to dress in your best attire.

Can Children Attend High Tea?

Yes, children are welcome to attend high tea, but be sure to check with the venue beforehand to see if they have any age restrictions.

What Kind of Tea is Served at High Tea?

A variety of teas are served at high tea, including black tea, green tea, herbal tea, and fruit tea. Be sure to try a few different types to find your favorite.
