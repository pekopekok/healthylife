Visit Iconic Landmarks

One of the best things to do in NYC Presidents Day weekend 2020 is to visit iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, and Central Park. You can take a ferry to Liberty Island and climb up the pedestal to get a stunning view of the city skyline. The Empire State Building offers a similar experience, where you can go up to the observation deck and admire the city from above. Central Park is a beautiful oasis in the middle of the city, where you can take a leisurely walk, have a picnic, or rent a bike to explore.

Explore Museums

New York City is home to some of the best museums in the world, such as the Metropolitan Museum of Art, American Museum of Natural History, and Museum of Modern Art. These museums have a vast collection of art, artifacts, and exhibits that showcase different aspects of history and culture. You can spend hours exploring these museums and learning something new.

Enjoy Live Theater

Broadway is synonymous with theater, and there is no better place to experience it than in New York City. During Presidents Day weekend 2020, you can catch some of the best shows on Broadway, such as Hamilton, The Lion King, and Wicked. If you prefer something more intimate, there are off-Broadway and off-off-Broadway shows that offer a different kind of experience.

Attend Live Music Shows

New York City has a vibrant music scene, and you can find live shows happening every night in different venues across the city. From jazz clubs to rock concerts, there is something for every music lover. During Presidents Day weekend 2020, you can catch some of the best acts performing at venues such as Carnegie Hall, Madison Square Garden, and Barclays Center.

Join Festive Events

New York City is known for its festivals and events, and Presidents Day weekend 2020 is no exception. There are parades, concerts, and other celebrations happening throughout the city.
