Things To Do Christmas Day In Las Vegas

Are you spending Christmas Day in Las Vegas this year? If so, you're in for a treat! While many businesses may be closed for the holiday, there are still plenty of things to do and see in Sin City. In this article, we'll cover some of the best activities and attractions to enjoy on Christmas Day in Las Vegas.

1. Visit the Bellagio Conservatory & Botanical Gardens

The Bellagio Conservatory & Botanical Gardens is a must-see attraction any time of year, but it's especially beautiful during the holidays. The Conservatory is transformed into a winter wonderland with thousands of twinkling lights, festive decorations, and stunning floral displays. Best of all, admission is free!

2. Take a Stroll Along the Las Vegas Strip

The Las Vegas Strip is always bustling with activity, and Christmas Day is no exception. Take a leisurely stroll along the Strip and enjoy the festive holiday decorations and lights. Be sure to stop by some of the iconic landmarks, like the Eiffel Tower at the Paris Las Vegas, the Fountains of Bellagio, and the Volcano at the Mirage.

3. Enjoy a Christmas Day Buffet

Many of the hotels and casinos on the Strip offer special Christmas Day buffets. Indulge in some traditional holiday fare, like turkey, ham, stuffing, and mashed potatoes, as well as some unique Vegas-style cuisine. Just be sure to make reservations in advance, as these buffets can fill up quickly.

4. See a Christmas Show

Las Vegas is known for its world-class entertainment, and Christmas Day is no exception. Many of the theaters and showrooms on the Strip offer special holiday shows featuring singers, dancers, and comedians. Check out the schedule and book your tickets in advance.

5. Visit the Ethel M Chocolate Factory

The Ethel M Chocolate Factory is located just outside of Las Vegas in Henderson, and it's a sweet destination any time of year. During the holidays, the factory is adorned with thousands of lights and decorations, and visitors can take a self-guided tour, sample some delicious chocolates, and even meet Santa Claus.

6. Go Ice Skating

Believe it or not, you can go ice skating in the desert! The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas offers an ice skating rink on its rooftop Boulevard Pool, complete with stunning views of the Strip. Skate rentals are available, and there's even a fire pit for roasting marshmallows.

7. Take a Day Trip to the Grand Canyon

If you're looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the Strip, consider taking a day trip to the Grand Canyon. There are plenty of tour companies that offer guided excursions to this natural wonder, and the stunning views are sure to take your breath away.

8. Shop at the Las Vegas Premium Outlets

The Las Vegas Premium Outlets are the perfect destination for holiday shopping. With over 150 stores, including designer brands like Michael Kors, Coach, and Kate Spade, you're sure to find something for everyone on your list. Plus, the outdoor mall is beautifully decorated for the holidays.

9. Relax at a Spa

After all the holiday hustle and bustle, you may be in need of some relaxation and pampering. Las Vegas is home to some of the most luxurious spas in the world, with treatments ranging from massages and facials to hydrotherapy and aromatherapy. Book a spa day and unwind in style.

10. Visit a Casino

Last but not least, you can't visit Las Vegas without trying your luck at a casino. Whether you're a seasoned gambler or a newbie, there's something for everyone. Many of the casinos offer special holiday promotions and events, so be sure to check out what's happening on Christmas Day.

There you have it – 10 things to do on Christmas Day in Las Vegas. Whether you're looking for festive holiday activities or just a relaxing day away from the crowds, there's something for everyone in Sin City. Happy holidays!
