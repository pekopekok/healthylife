Description

The Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu is a traditional English tea service offered at the Ritz Hotel in London. The Ritz Hotel is a luxury hotel located in the heart of London, and the Afternoon Tea Menu is a popular choice for visitors and locals alike.

The Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu includes a selection of sandwiches, scones with clotted cream and jam, and a selection of cakes and pastries. The tea is served in the hotel's elegant Palm Court, where guests can enjoy the beautiful surroundings and the live music provided by the hotel's resident pianist.

The Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu is a quintessentially English experience, and one that should not be missed if you are visiting London.

How to

If you want to experience the Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu, there are a few things you need to know:

  • You need to make a reservation in advance
  • The dress code is smart casual – no jeans or sportswear
  • The Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu is served daily from 11:30am to 7:30pm
  • Prices start from £53 per person

Step by step

If you want to experience the Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu, here is what you need to do:

  1. Visit the Ritz Hotel website
  2. Select the date and time you would like to visit
  3. Select the number of people in your party
  4. Choose any special dietary requirements
  5. Enter your contact details
  6. Pay for your reservation
  7. Arrive at the Ritz Hotel at the specified time
  8. Enjoy your Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu experience!

Tips

If you want to make the most of your Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu experience, here are a few tips:

  • Arrive early to take in the beautiful surroundings
  • Try the Ritz Blend tea – it is specially created for the hotel
  • Ask the staff for recommendations if you are unsure what to order
  • Take your time – the experience is meant to be enjoyed at a leisurely pace
  • Bring a camera – the Palm Court is a beautiful setting for photos

Solution

If you are looking for a unique and memorable experience in London, the Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu is the perfect choice. With its beautiful surroundings, delicious food, and live music, it is a quintessentially English experience that should not be missed.

Whether you are visiting London for the first time or you are a local looking for a special treat, the Ritz Afternoon Tea Menu is sure to impress.
