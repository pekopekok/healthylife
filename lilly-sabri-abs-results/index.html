If you want to get toned abs like Lilly Sabri, you need to follow a few simple steps. First, you need to focus on your diet. Second, you need to incorporate cardio into your workout routine. Cardio exercises like running, cycling, and swimming can help you burn fat and reveal your abs. You should aim to do cardio for at least 30 minutes a day, five days a week.

Third, you need to do targeted abs exercises that engage all the muscles in your core. Lilly Sabri's abs workout is a great place to start. Her workout routine includes exercises like crunches, planks, and Russian twists that can help you tone your abs and strengthen your core.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting toned abs like Lilly Sabri:

  1. Eat a balanced diet that is rich in protein, fiber, and healthy fats.
  2. Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and avoid bloating.
  3. Incorporate cardio into your workout routine for at least 30 minutes a day, five days a week.
  4. Do targeted abs exercises that engage all the muscles in your core.
  5. Follow Lilly Sabri's abs workout routine.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results from your abs workout:
