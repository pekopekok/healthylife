Things To Do During The Day In Las Vegas

Written by Bowie Aug 08, 2023 · 4 min read

.

Las Vegas is known for its lively nightlife, but there are plenty of things to do during the day as well. Whether you're traveling with family, friends or solo, there's something for everyone. In this article, we'll explore some of the best things to do during the day in Las Vegas.

Table of Contents

  • Visit the Strip
  • Explore the Museums
  • Go Shopping
  • Visit the Hoover Dam
  • Take a Helicopter Tour
  • Visit the Red Rock Canyon

  • Take a Food Tour
  • Go Golfing

Visit the Strip

The Las Vegas Strip is a must-visit attraction during the day. Take a stroll along the boulevard and marvel at the towering hotels, casinos and attractions. You can visit the Bellagio fountains, watch the volcano eruption at The Mirage or visit the High Roller Observation Wheel for a bird's eye view of the city.

Explore the Museums

Las Vegas has a range of museums that cater to a variety of interests. The Mob Museum is a popular choice for those interested in the history of organized crime. The Neon Museum is a great option for those who want to learn about the history of Las Vegas' iconic neon signs. And for those who love cars, the Shelby American Collection is a must-visit.

Go Shopping

Las Vegas is a shopper's paradise, with a range of options to suit all budgets. From luxury shopping at the Forum Shops at Caesars Palace to bargain hunting at the Las Vegas North Premium Outlets, there's something for everyone. Don't forget to visit the Fashion Show Mall for a unique shopping experience that includes runway shows and celebrity appearances.

Visit the Hoover Dam

Just a short drive from Las Vegas is the Hoover Dam, an engineering marvel that attracts millions of visitors each year. Take a tour of the dam and learn about its history and construction, or take a scenic helicopter tour for a bird's eye view of the dam and the surrounding area.

Take a Helicopter Tour

Speaking of helicopter tours, Las Vegas is a great place to take one. You can fly over the city's iconic landmarks, such as the Hoover Dam, Red Rock Canyon and the Las Vegas Strip. It's a unique and unforgettable way to see the city from above.

Visit the Red Rock Canyon

For outdoor enthusiasts, the Red Rock Canyon is a must-visit attraction. Just a short drive from the city, the canyon offers hiking, rock climbing and scenic drives. You can also take a guided tour of the canyon to learn about its history and geology.

Enjoy a Spa Day

Las Vegas is home to some of the best spas in the world, offering a range of treatments and services to help you relax and rejuvenate. From traditional massages and facials to unique experiences like the Himalayan Salt Stone Massage, you're sure to find something that suits your needs.

Take a Food Tour

Las Vegas is a foodie's paradise, with a range of restaurants and cuisines to suit all tastes. Take a food tour to sample some of the city's best dishes, or book a table at one of the many celebrity chef restaurants for a unique dining experience.

Go Golfing

Las Vegas is home to some of the best golf courses in the world, with stunning views and challenging courses. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a beginner, there's a course that caters to your skill level.

Visit the Ethel M Chocolate Factory

If you have a sweet tooth, the Ethel M Chocolate Factory is a must-visit attraction. Take a tour of the factory and see how chocolate is made, or sample some of the delicious treats in the gift shop. Don't forget to visit the cactus garden for a unique desert experience.

Conclusion

Las Vegas has a lot to offer during the day, from iconic landmarks to unique experiences. Whether you're visiting for the first time or the hundredth, there's always something new to discover. So go ahead and explore the city, and don't forget to have fun!
