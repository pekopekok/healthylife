Introduction

Are you wondering what time to pray Tahajjud? This article will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to pray Tahajjud and what time to pray it.

What is Tahajjud?

Tahajjud is a voluntary prayer that is performed at night after Isha prayer and before Fajr prayer. It is considered to be one of the most rewarding prayers and is highly recommended in Islam.

How to Pray Tahajjud

The Tahajjud prayer consists of at least two Rak’ah (units of prayer) and can be performed in multiples of two. Here is a step by step guide on how to pray Tahajjud:

Step 1: Make Wudu

Perform Wudu (ablution) to purify yourself before prayer.

Step 2: Intention

Make the intention to perform Tahajjud prayer.

Step 3: Recitation

Recite the opening supplication (dua) and then recite any Surah or verses from the Quran that you know. You can also recite the recommended Surahs for Tahajjud, which include Surah Al-Fatiha, Surah Al-Ala, and Surah Al-Qadr.

Step 4: Ruku and Sujood

Perform Ruku (bowing) and Sujood (prostration) as you would in any other prayer. Repeat this for at least two Rak’ahs.

What Time to Pray Tahajjud

The best time to perform Tahajjud prayer is during the last third of the night, which is the time when Allah (SWT) descends to the lower heaven to listen to the prayers of His servants. This time is calculated by dividing the time between Maghrib and Fajr into three parts and performing Tahajjud in the last third of the night.

However, if you are unable to wake up during the last third of the night, you can perform Tahajjud anytime after Isha prayer and before Fajr prayer.

Tips for Praying Tahajjud

Here are some tips to help you perform Tahajjud prayer:

  • Try to sleep early and wake up early to perform Tahajjud.
  • Set an alarm or ask someone to wake you up for Tahajjud.
  • Recite Surah Al-Fatiha, Surah Al-Ala, and Surah Al-Qadr in Tahajjud.
  • Make sincere dua (supplication) during Tahajjud.
  • Keep a consistent routine of performing Tahajjud.

FAQ

Q: Can Tahajjud be performed after Fajr prayer?

A: No, Tahajjud cannot be performed after Fajr prayer. It can only be performed after Isha prayer and before Fajr prayer.

Q: How many Rak’ahs are recommended for Tahajjud?

A: At least two Rak’ahs are recommended for Tahajjud, but it can be performed in multiples of two.

Conclusion

Performing Tahajjud prayer is highly recommended in Islam and is considered to be a means of getting closer to Allah (SWT). It is important to know what time to perform Tahajjud and how to perform it properly. By following the steps and tips in this guide, you can start performing Tahajjud prayer and reap its rewards.
