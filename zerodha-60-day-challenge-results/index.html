If you are an avid trader or investor, you must have heard of the Zerodha 60 Day Challenge. It is a unique initiative by Zerodha, one of India's leading discount brokers, that rewards traders who can show consistent profits for 60 trading days. In this post, we will discuss the Zerodha 60 Day Challenge Results for the year 2023, how to participate in the challenge, and everything in between.

What is the Zerodha 60 Day Challenge?

The Zerodha 60 Day Challenge is a program that encourages traders to test their skills and knowledge of the stock market. The challenge requires participants to show consistent profits for 60 trading days, and the rewards are substantial. The challenge is open to all Zerodha clients, and there is no entry fee.

How to Participate in the Zerodha 60 Day Challenge?

Participating in the Zerodha 60 Day Challenge is easy. Here are the steps:

Step 1:

Open a trading account with Zerodha if you don't have one already.

Step 2:

Login to your account and go to the 60 Day Challenge page.

Step 3:

Click on 'Register' to enroll in the challenge.

Step 4:

Start trading and aim for consistent profits for 60 trading days.

Zerodha 60 Day Challenge Results 2023

The Zerodha 60 Day Challenge Results for the year 2023 have not been announced yet. However, based on the previous years' results, we can expect some exciting rewards for the winners. The top 3 traders who show consistent profits for 60 trading days will receive cash prizes worth Rs. 1 Lakh, Rs. 75,000, and Rs. 50,000, respectively.

Tips to Ace the Zerodha 60 Day Challenge

Participating in the Zerodha 60 Day Challenge can be a great learning experience for traders. Here are some tips to help you ace the challenge:

Tip 1:

Develop a trading plan and stick to it.

Tip 2:

Practice risk management and avoid over-trading.

Tip 3:

Stay updated with the latest news and trends in the stock market.

Pros and Cons of the Zerodha 60 Day Challenge

Like any other initiative, the Zerodha 60 Day Challenge has its pros and cons. Here are some of them:

Pros:

  • No entry fee
  • Opportunity to test trading skills
  • Substantial cash prizes for the winners
  • Great learning experience

Cons:

  • Requires a significant amount of time and effort
  • Risk of losing money
  • Can be stressful
