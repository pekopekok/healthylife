What is a Weight Gain Veg Diet Chart?

A weight gain veg diet chart is a plan that includes foods that are high in calories, protein, and healthy fats. These foods help in gaining weight and muscle mass in a healthy way. The diet chart is designed in such a way that it provides all the necessary nutrients required for the body to function properly.

How to Create a Weight Gain Veg Diet Chart?

Creating a weight gain veg diet chart is easy. You need to choose foods that are high in calories, protein, and healthy fats. Here is a sample weight gain veg diet chart in Hindi:

MealFood Items
Early MorningA glass of warm milk with 2-3 dates and 2-3 almonds
Breakfast2-3 parathas with paneer bhurji or chana masala, a glass of fresh fruit juice
Mid-MorningA bowl of sprouts or a fruit salad with honey and nuts
Lunch2-3 rotis with dal, sabzi, and curd, a bowl of brown rice, and a side salad
Evening SnackA smoothie with banana, peanut butter, and milk or a plate of poha with peanuts and veggies
Dinner2-3 rotis with paneer or tofu gravy, a bowl of dal, and a side salad
Bedtime SnackA glass of warm milk with a pinch of turmeric and honey

Step-by-Step Guide to Follow the Weight Gain Veg Diet Chart

Here is a step-by-step guide to follow the weight gain veg diet chart:

  1. Start your day with a glass of warm milk with dates and almonds.
  2. Eat a high-calorie breakfast that includes parathas with paneer bhurji or chana masala and a glass of fresh fruit juice.
  3. Have a bowl of sprouts or a fruit salad with honey and nuts for mid-morning snack.
  4. Eat a balanced lunch that includes rotis with dal, sabzi, and curd, a bowl of brown rice, and a side salad.
  5. Have a smoothie with banana, peanut butter, and milk or a plate of poha with peanuts and veggies for the evening snack.
  6. Eat a light dinner that includes rotis with paneer or tofu gravy, a bowl of dal, and a side salad.
  7. End your day with a glass of warm milk with turmeric and honey.

Tips to Follow a Weight Gain Veg Diet Chart

Here are some tips to follow a weight gain veg diet chart:

  • Eat small and frequent meals throughout the day.
  • Avoid junk food and sugary drinks.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Incorporate weight training exercises in your workout routine to build muscle mass.
  • Get enough sleep to allow your body to rest and recover.

Solution to Common Problems While Following a Weight Gain Veg Diet Chart

Here are some solutions to common problems while following a weight gain veg diet chart:

  • If you are finding it difficult to eat large meals, try eating smaller meals more frequently.
  • If you are not able to consume enough protein through your diet, consider taking protein supplements.
  • If you are not able to follow the diet chart consistently, try meal prepping and planning your meals in advance.

FAQs

Q. Can I follow the weight gain veg diet chart if I have food allergies?

A. Yes, you can modify the diet chart according to your food allergies and preferences.

Q. How long do I need to follow the weight gain veg diet chart to see results?

A. You need to follow the diet chart consistently for at least 4-6 weeks to see significant results.

Pros and Cons of a Weight Gain Veg Diet Chart

Pros

  • Provides all the necessary nutrients required for the body to function properly
  • Can be modified according to individual preferences and food allergies

Cons

  • May require additional protein supplements for some individuals
