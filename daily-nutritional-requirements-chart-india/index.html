Do you want to know how to meet your daily nutritional requirements in India? This article provides a comprehensive guide to achieving a healthy and balanced diet that meets the nutritional needs of your body. We will cover the essential nutrients, their functions, and the recommended daily intake for different age groups. We will also provide practical tips on how to plan your meals and make healthy food choices.

What are Nutrients?

Nutrients are the building blocks of a healthy diet. They are essential for growth, repair, and maintenance of the body. There are six main nutrients that our body needs:

  • Carbohydrates
  • Proteins
  • Fats
  • Vitamins
  • Minerals
  • Water

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are the primary source of energy for the body. They are found in grains, fruits, vegetables, and dairy products. The recommended daily intake of carbohydrates for adults is 130 grams. However, the intake may vary depending on an individual's physical activity level and body weight.

Proteins

Proteins are responsible for building and repairing tissues in the body. They are found in meat, fish, poultry, beans, and nuts. The recommended daily intake of proteins for adults is 0.8 grams per kilogram of body weight.

Fats

Fats provide energy and help in the absorption of vitamins. They are found in oils, nuts, seeds, and dairy products. The recommended daily intake of fats for adults is around 20-35% of the total calorie intake.

Vitamins and Minerals

Vitamins and minerals are essential for the proper functioning of the body. They are found in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and dairy products. The recommended daily intake of vitamins and minerals varies depending on the age, gender, and physical activity level of an individual.

Water

Water is essential for the body to function properly. It helps in digestion, absorption, and transport of nutrients. The recommended daily intake of water is around 3-4 liters for men and 2-3 liters for women.

How to Plan a Healthy Meal?

Planning a healthy meal is essential to meet your daily nutritional requirements. Here are some tips on how to plan a healthy meal:

  • Include a variety of fruits and vegetables in your diet
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Include lean proteins in your diet
  • Choose healthy fats like nuts, seeds, and olive oil
  • Limit your intake of processed foods and sugary drinks
  • Drink plenty of water

Pros

Following a daily nutritional requirements chart in India is essential for maintaining a healthy and balanced diet.
