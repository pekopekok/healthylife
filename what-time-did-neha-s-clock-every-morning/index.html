What if I can't wake up on time even with an alarm clock?

If you are having trouble waking up on time, there may be an underlying issue that is causing your fatigue. Most adults need between 7-9 hours of sleep per night. To make sure you are getting enough sleep, establish a consistent sleep schedule and practice good sleep hygiene.
