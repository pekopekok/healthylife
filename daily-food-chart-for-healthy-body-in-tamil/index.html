If you are looking for a healthy and balanced diet plan, you are in the right place. In this article, we will provide you with a daily food chart for a healthy body in Tamil. This chart will help you plan your meals and ensure that you consume all the necessary nutrients for a healthy body. Let's get started!

Breakfast

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day. It is important to start your day with a nutritious and healthy breakfast. Here is a daily food chart for a healthy breakfast:

Food ItemQuantity
Idli or Dosa2-3
Sambar1 bowl
Chutney1-2 tablespoons
Boiled Egg1

Mid-Morning Snack

It is important to have a mid-morning snack to keep your energy levels up until lunch. Here is a healthy snack you can have:

  • Fruits (Banana/Apple/Papaya/Orange)
  • Almonds/Walnuts
  • Buttermilk

Lunch

Lunch is an important meal of the day. It is important to have a nutritious and balanced meal for lunch. Here is a daily food chart for a healthy lunch:

Food ItemQuantity
Brown Rice1 cup
Sambar/Rasam1 bowl
Vegetable Curry (Beans/Carrot/Brinjal/Potato)1 bowl
Salad (Cucumber/Tomato/Onion)1 bowl
Curd1 bowl

Mid-Afternoon Snack

It is important to have a mid-afternoon snack to keep your energy levels up until dinner. Here is a healthy snack you can have:

  • Fruits (Grapes/Watermelon/Pineapple)
  • Murukku (in moderation)
  • Tea/Coffee (without sugar)

Dinner

Dinner should be a light meal. Here is a daily food chart for a healthy dinner:

Food ItemQuantity
Chapati/Roti2-3
Vegetable Curry (Beans/Carrot/Brinjal/Potato)1 bowl
Salad (Cucumber/Tomato/Onion)1 bowl
Buttermilk1 glass

Tips for a Healthy Diet

  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day
  • Avoid processed and junk food
  • Include a variety of fruits and vegetables in your diet
  • Avoid eating late at night
  • Limit your sugar and salt intake

FAQs

What should I drink for a healthy body?

You should drink plenty of water, buttermilk, and fresh fruit juices without added sugar for a healthy body.

Can I eat junk food in moderation?

While it is okay to eat junk food in moderation, it is important to limit your intake and prioritize healthy food options.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • A healthy diet can help prevent diseases
  • A balanced diet can improve your mood and energy levels
  • Eating healthy can promote weight loss

Cons:

  • Healthy food options can be expensive
  • It can be challenging to stick to a healthy diet plan
  • It can be time-consuming to plan and prepare healthy meals
