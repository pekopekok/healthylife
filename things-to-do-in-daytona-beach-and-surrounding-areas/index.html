Things To Do In Daytona Beach And Surrounding Areas

If you're looking for a fun and relaxing vacation, Daytona Beach and the surrounding areas are perfect destinations. With miles of sandy beaches, a vibrant nightlife, and plenty of outdoor activities, there's something for everyone. In this article, we'll introduce you to some of the best things to do in Daytona Beach and the surrounding areas.

Table of Contents

  • Beaches
  • Outdoor Activities
  • Nightlife

Beaches

Daytona Beach is known for its stunning beaches, and there are plenty of them to choose from. One of the most popular is Daytona Beach itself, which is known for its wide, white sand beach and clear blue water. If you're looking for something a bit quieter, head to New Smyrna Beach, which is known for its calm waters and laid-back atmosphere.

Other great beaches in the area include Ormond Beach, which is great for surfing, and Ponce Inlet, which is known for its beautiful views and great fishing.

Outdoor Activities

If you're looking for outdoor activities, Daytona Beach and the surrounding areas have plenty to offer. One of the most popular is the Daytona International Speedway, which hosts the Daytona 500 every year. You can take a tour of the speedway, or even drive on the track yourself.

For something a bit more relaxing, head to the Lighthouse Point Park and Museum. Here you can climb to the top of the lighthouse for stunning views of the area, or enjoy a picnic in the park.

If you're looking for something more adventurous, head to Blue Springs State Park, where you can go tubing down the river, or even swim with manatees.

Nightlife

Daytona Beach is known for its vibrant nightlife, with plenty of bars, clubs, and restaurants to choose from. One of the most popular spots is Ocean Deck Beach Club, which is known for its live music and great drinks. Another great spot is Razzle's Nightclub, which has multiple dance floors and a variety of music.

If you're looking for something a bit more low-key, head to the Daytona Beach Boardwalk, where you can enjoy a stroll along the beach while listening to live music or grabbing a drink at one of the beach bars.

Museums

If you're interested in history or art, Daytona Beach and the surrounding areas have plenty of museums to offer. One of the most popular is the Museum of Arts and Sciences, which has a wide variety of exhibits, from prehistoric fossils to contemporary art.

Another great museum is the Halifax Historical Museum, which tells the story of Daytona Beach and the surrounding area. Here you can learn about the area's rich history, from the Native American tribes who lived here to the early settlers who made their home on the beach.

Shopping

If you're looking for some retail therapy, Daytona Beach and the surrounding areas have plenty of shopping opportunities. One of the most popular spots is the Tanger Outlets, which has over 70 stores, including Nike, Polo Ralph Lauren, and Coach.

Another great spot is the Daytona Beach Boardwalk, which has a variety of shops and boutiques. Here you can find everything from souvenirs to beachwear.

Tips for Visiting Daytona Beach and Surrounding Areas

  • Be sure to bring sunscreen and plenty of water, as the Florida sun can be intense.
  • If you're visiting during peak season, be prepared for crowds and higher prices.
  • If you're planning on visiting the Daytona International Speedway, be sure to check the schedule ahead of time to see if there are any races or events happening.
  • If you're looking for a quieter beach experience, consider visiting during the off-season.
  • Be sure to try some of the local seafood, which is some of the freshest and most delicious in the country.

Pros and Cons of Visiting Daytona Beach and Surrounding Areas

Pros:

  • Beautiful beaches
  • Plenty of outdoor activities
  • Vibrant nightlife
  • Rich history and culture

Cons:

  • Can be crowded and expensive during peak season
  • The Florida sun can be intense

Overall, Daytona Beach and the surrounding areas are great destinations for a fun and relaxing vacation. With so much to see and do, you're sure to have a great time.
