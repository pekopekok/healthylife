Description

Have you ever wondered whether morning 8 AM is considered as AM or PM? The answer is quite simple - morning 8 AM is considered as AM. The term 'AM' stands for 'Ante Meridiem,' which means 'before noon.' On the other hand, 'PM' stands for 'Post Meridiem,' which means 'afternoon.' Therefore, morning 8 AM falls under the 'AM' category as it is before noon.

How to

To understand whether morning 8 AM is AM or PM, you need to know the difference between the two. AM and PM are used to differentiate between the time of the day. AM refers to the time period between midnight and noon, while PM refers to the time period between noon and midnight. Therefore, morning 8 AM falls under the AM category.

Step by step

Step 1: Know the difference between AM and PM.
Step 2: Understand that morning 8 AM falls under the AM category as it is before noon.
