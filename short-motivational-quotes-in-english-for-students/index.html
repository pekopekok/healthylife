Are you feeling down and need some inspiration? Are you struggling with school work or feeling unmotivated? Fear not! In this article, we have compiled a list of short motivational quotes in English for students. These quotes will help you feel inspired and motivated to tackle any challenge that comes your way. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Believe in yourself
  • Success is not final
  • Don't give up
  • Push yourself
  • Be the change
  • Stay positive
  • Take action
  • Embrace failure
  • Strive for progress
  • Never stop learning

Believe in yourself

"Believe you can and you're halfway there." - Theodore Roosevelt

This quote reminds us that having faith in ourselves and our abilities is essential to achieving success. When we truly believe in ourselves, we are more likely to take risks and push ourselves out of our comfort zones.


Success is not final

"Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts." - Winston Churchill

This quote reminds us that success is not the end-all-be-all. We must continue to work hard and push ourselves even after achieving success. Failure is not the end either, but rather an opportunity to learn and grow.


Don't give up

"Don't watch the clock; do what it does. Keep going." - Sam Levenson

This quote reminds us to keep pushing forward and not give up, even when things get tough. Time will pass regardless of whether or not we choose to work towards our goals, so we might as well keep going.


Push yourself

"If you want to achieve greatness, stop asking for permission." - Unknown

This quote reminds us that we are in control of our own success. We must push ourselves and take ownership of our goals in order to achieve greatness.


Be the change

"Be the change you wish to see in the world." - Mahatma Gandhi

This quote reminds us that we have the power to make a difference in the world. We must take action and be the change we wish to see.


Stay positive

"Positive anything is better than negative nothing." - Elbert Hubbard

This quote reminds us that staying positive is essential to our success. We must focus on the good and stay optimistic, even during difficult times.


Take action

"The future depends on what you do today." - Mahatma Gandhi

This quote reminds us that we must take action if we want to achieve our goals. We cannot wait for the perfect moment or for someone else to do it for us.


Embrace failure

"I have not failed. I've just found 10,000 ways that won't work." - Thomas Edison

This quote reminds us that failure is not the end, but rather an opportunity to learn and grow. We must embrace failure and use it as a stepping stone towards success.


Strive for progress

"Strive for progress, not perfection." - Unknown

This quote reminds us that perfection is not attainable, but progress is. We must focus on making progress towards our goals, rather than striving for perfection.


Never stop learning

"Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire." - William Butler Yeats

This quote reminds us that learning is a lifelong journey. We must never stop seeking knowledge and expanding our horizons.


Conclusion

We hope these short motivational quotes in English for students have inspired and motivated you to work towards your goals. Remember to believe in yourself, stay positive, take action, and never stop learning. With hard work and dedication, you can achieve anything you set your mind to!
