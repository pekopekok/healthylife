If you're looking for a way to achieve glowing skin, you might be surprised to learn that what you drink in the morning can have a significant impact. In this guide, we'll explore the best drinks to consume in the morning for healthy, glowing skin. We'll discuss the benefits of each drink, how to make them, and any potential downsides. So, let's dive in!

Water

When it comes to glowing skin, water is the most important drink to consume in the morning. Water helps to flush out toxins from the body, which can lead to clearer, healthier skin. It also keeps the skin hydrated, which can reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

The best way to consume water in the morning is to drink it as soon as you wake up. Aim for at least 8-10 glasses throughout the day to keep your skin looking its best.

How to Make It:

Simply pour a glass of water and drink it as soon as you wake up in the morning.

Pros:

  • Easy to make
  • Inexpensive
  • Hydrates the skin

Cons:

  • None

Green Tea

Green tea is packed with antioxidants, which can help to protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals. It also contains anti-inflammatory properties, which can reduce redness and inflammation in the skin.

The best way to consume green tea in the morning is to brew a cup and drink it while it's still hot.

How to Make It:

Boil water and let it cool for a few minutes. Pour the hot water over a green tea bag and let it steep for 3-5 minutes. Remove the tea bag and enjoy!

Pros:

  • Packed with antioxidants
  • Reduces inflammation
  • May reduce the risk of skin cancer

Cons:

  • Contains caffeine, which can be dehydrating
  • May stain teeth

Lemon Water

Lemon water is a great way to start your day. It's packed with vitamin C, which can help to brighten the skin and reduce the appearance of dark spots. It also has detoxifying properties, which can help to flush out toxins from the body.

The best way to consume lemon water in the morning is to squeeze half a lemon into a glass of water and drink it on an empty stomach.

How to Make It:

Squeeze half a lemon into a glass of water and drink it on an empty stomach.

Pros:

  • Packed with vitamin C
  • Detoxifying properties
  • May help to reduce the appearance of dark spots

Cons:

  • Lemon juice can be acidic, which may irritate sensitive skin
  • May erode tooth enamel over time

Celery Juice

Celery juice has become a popular drink for its health benefits, including its ability to improve skin health. It's packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which can help to reduce inflammation and promote healthy, glowing skin.

The best way to consume celery juice in the morning is to juice fresh celery stalks and drink the juice on an empty stomach.

How to Make It:

Wash and chop a few celery stalks. Juice the celery and drink the juice on an empty stomach.

Pros:

  • Packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants
  • May reduce inflammation in the skin
  • May improve digestion

Cons:

  • May cause stomach upset in some people
  • May interact with certain medications

Aloe Vera Juice

Aloe vera juice is known for its skin-healing properties. It contains vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which can help to soothe and hydrate the skin.

The best way to consume aloe vera juice in the morning is to drink it on an empty stomach.

How to Make It:

Purchase pure aloe vera juice from a health food store or online. Drink the recommended amount on an empty stomach.

Pros:

  • Contains skin-healing properties
  • Hydrates the skin
  • May reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles

Cons:

  • May cause stomach upset in some people
  • May interact with certain medications

The best way to consume carrot juice in the morning is to juice fresh carrots and drink the juice on an empty stomach.

How to Make It:

Wash and chop a few carrots. Juice the carrots and drink the juice on an empty stomach.

Pros:

  • May protect the skin from damage caused by the sun
  • May promote healthy, glowing skin

Cons:

  • May cause stomach upset in some people
  • May interact with certain medications

Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar is known for its numerous health benefits, including its ability to improve skin health. It contains acetic acid, which can help to exfoliate the skin and reduce the appearance of dark spots.

The best way to consume apple cider vinegar in the morning is to mix a tablespoon with a glass of water and drink it on an empty stomach.

How to Make It:

Mix a tablespoon of apple cider vinegar with a glass of water and drink it on an empty stomach.

Pros:

  • May reduce the appearance of dark spots
  • May improve digestion
