Things To Do In Daytona Beach Fl When It Rains

Daytona Beach, Florida is known for its sunshine, sand, and surf. However, when the weather takes a turn for the worse, there are still plenty of things to do in Daytona Beach FL when it rains. Don't let a little rain dampen your vacation spirits. Here are some of the top things to do in Daytona Beach FL when it rains.

Indoor Attractions

When it's raining outside, the best place to be is indoors. Daytona Beach FL has a number of indoor attractions that are perfect for a rainy day. Visit the Daytona Beach Boardwalk and Pier, which has a number of indoor attractions including an arcade, shops, and restaurants. The Daytona Lagoon Water Park is also a great option, with indoor water slides, wave pool, and laser tag arena.

Museums and Art Galleries

If you're looking for something educational and cultural to do in Daytona Beach FL when it rains, check out some of the local museums and art galleries. The Museum of Arts and Sciences has exhibits on art, science, and history. The Southeast Museum of Photography has rotating exhibits of contemporary photography. The Cici and Hyatt Brown Museum of Art has an extensive collection of Florida art and history.

Shopping

Rainy days are the perfect time to do some shopping. Daytona Beach FL has a number of shopping options, including the Tanger Outlets, Volusia Mall, and the Daytona Flea and Farmers Market. You can also check out the local antique shops and boutiques for unique finds.

Movie Theaters

When all else fails, catch a movie at one of Daytona Beach FL's movie theaters. The Regal Cinemas Pavilion 14 and XD and the Cinematique Theater both offer a variety of movies for all ages.

Bowling Alleys

Another fun indoor activity for a rainy day in Daytona Beach FL is bowling. The Sunshine Bowling Center and the Bellair Lanes both offer bowling lanes, arcade games, and food and drinks.

Spas and Relaxation

Don't let the rainy weather stress you out. Take some time to relax and pamper yourself at one of Daytona Beach FL's spas. The Ocean Waters Spa and the Indulge Spa offer massages, facials, and other spa treatments.

Indoor Sports

If you're looking for some indoor sports activities, Daytona Beach FL has a number of options. The Daytona Beach Ice Arena offers ice skating and hockey. The Daytona Indoor Kart Racing has go-kart racing and arcade games. The Daytona Beach Golf Club has indoor golf simulators.

Escape Rooms

For a fun and challenging indoor activity, try one of Daytona Beach FL's escape rooms. The Daytona Escape Room Experience and the Escape Room Adventure of Daytona both offer immersive escape room experiences.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of things to do in Daytona Beach FL when it rains. Whether you're in the mood for indoor attractions, museums and art galleries, shopping, movie theaters, bowling alleys, spas and relaxation, indoor sports, or escape rooms, Daytona Beach FL has something for everyone. Don't let a little rain ruin your vacation. Embrace it and have some fun!
