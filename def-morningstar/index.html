Description

Def Morningstar is a financial information company that provides data and research to investors, including mutual fund ratings, stock analysis, and market trends. The company was founded in 1984 by Joe Mansueto and is headquartered in Chicago, Illinois. Def Morningstar is well-known for its fund ratings, which provide an objective assessment of a fund's past performance, management, and expenses.

Def Morningstar offers a range of products and services that can be used by individual investors, financial advisors, and institutional investors.
