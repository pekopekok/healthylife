Introduction

London is one of the most vibrant and exciting cities in the world, with countless activities for visitors and locals alike. However, if you're looking for a fun and relaxed way to spend your day, day drinking might be just the thing for you. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best day drinking activities in London, from pub crawls to rooftop bars to wine tasting and more.

Pub Crawls

One of the most popular day drinking activities in London is the classic pub crawl. There are countless pubs and bars to choose from, so you can tailor your crawl to your own preferences. Some popular areas for pub crawls include Shoreditch, Camden, and Soho. You can either plan your own route or join a guided tour, which can be a great way to meet new people and discover hidden gems.

Rooftop Bars

London is home to some of the best rooftop bars in the world, offering stunning views of the city skyline. Some popular options include Sushisamba in the City, Pergola Paddington, and the Queen of Hoxton in Shoreditch. These bars can get busy, especially on weekends, so be sure to arrive early if you want to secure a good spot.

Beer Gardens

London has a great selection of beer gardens, perfect for enjoying a pint in the sunshine. Some popular options include The Albion in Islington, The Prince in West Brompton, and The Ship in Wandsworth. Many beer gardens also offer food and snacks to accompany your drinks, so you can make a day of it.

Wine Tasting

If wine is more your thing, London has plenty of options for wine tasting. Many wine bars offer tasting sessions, where you can sample a selection of wines and learn about their origins and flavours. Some popular wine bars in London include Vinoteca in Marylebone, The Remedy in Fitzrovia, and Terroirs in Covent Garden.

Cocktail Making Classes

For something a bit more interactive, why not try a cocktail making class? Many bars and cocktail lounges in London offer classes where you can learn how to make your own cocktails under the guidance of an expert mixologist. Some popular options include The London Cocktail Club, The Alchemist, and The Gibson.

Boat Tours

If you want to combine your day drinking with a bit of sightseeing, a boat tour might be just the thing for you. There are several companies offering river cruises along the Thames, where you can enjoy a drink and take in the city's iconic landmarks from a unique perspective. Some popular options include City Cruises and Thames Clippers.

Pros and Cons of Day Drinking

Pros

  • It's a fun and relaxed way to spend your day.
  • You can discover new bars and pubs.
  • You can meet new people and make new friends.
  • You can enjoy the sunshine and fresh air.

Cons

  • You might end up drinking too much and feeling hungover the next day.
  • It can be expensive, especially in popular areas.
  • You might encounter rowdy or unpleasant people.

FAQs

Is day drinking legal in London?

Yes, day drinking is legal in London, as long as you're of legal drinking age (18 or over) and not causing a disturbance or putting yourself or others in danger.

What's the best day of the week for day drinking?

Weekends are generally the most popular days for day drinking, but many bars and pubs offer specials during the week as well.

Conclusion

Day drinking can be a fun and enjoyable way to spend your time in London, whether you're a local or a visitor. From pub crawls to rooftop bars to wine tasting and more, there's no shortage of activities to choose from. Just remember to drink responsibly and have fun!
