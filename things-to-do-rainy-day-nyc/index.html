Things To Do On A Rainy Day In Nyc

When the rain comes down in New York City, you might be tempted to stay inside and watch TV all day. However, there are plenty of fun things to do on a rainy day in NYC that will keep you entertained and dry. Below we've listed some of the best activities and attractions you can enjoy when the weather is less than ideal.

1. Visit a Museum

NYC is home to some of the most incredible museums in the world, so why not spend a rainy day exploring one of them? The American Museum of Natural History, the Metropolitan Museum of Art, and the Museum of Modern Art are just a few of the options available. Not only will you stay dry, but you'll also learn something new and fascinating.

2. Catch a Movie

Going to the movies is a classic rainy day activity for a reason. NYC has plenty of great movie theaters to choose from, including the historic Ziegfeld Theater and the Alamo Drafthouse in Brooklyn. Grab some popcorn and settle in for a few hours of entertainment.

3. Go Shopping

If you're looking to stay dry and get some retail therapy, head to one of NYC's many shopping destinations. The iconic Macy's department store, the luxurious Saks Fifth Avenue, and the trendy SoHo neighborhood are just a few of the options available. You might even find a great deal on a rainy day!

4. Check Out a Bookstore

NYC is home to some of the best independent bookstores in the country. Spend a rainy day browsing the shelves at The Strand, McNally Jackson, or Housing Works Bookstore Cafe. You might even find a new favorite author or book.

5. See a Broadway Show

NYC's theater scene is legendary, and there's no better time to see a show than on a rainy day. From classic musicals like The Phantom of the Opera to new productions like Hamilton, there's something for everyone on Broadway. Plus, you'll stay dry and entertained for a few hours.

6. Visit an Aquarium

If you're looking for a unique rainy day activity, head to the New York Aquarium in Brooklyn. You'll be able to see sea lions, sharks, and even penguins up close. It's a great option for families with kids, but adults will enjoy it too.

7. Have a Spa Day

What better way to spend a rainy day than getting pampered at a spa? NYC has plenty of options to choose from, including Bliss Spa, Aire Ancient Baths, and The Red Door. Treat yourself to a massage, facial, or other relaxing treatment.

8. Take a Cooking Class

If you're a foodie, spending a rainy day in a cooking class could be just what you need. NYC has plenty of options available, from Italian cooking classes at Eataly to sushi-making classes at Morimoto. You'll learn new skills and get to eat some delicious food.

9. Visit a Botanical Garden

Escape the rain and head to the Brooklyn Botanic Garden or the New York Botanical Garden in the Bronx. You'll be able to see beautiful flowers, plants, and trees, and maybe even learn a thing or two about gardening.

10. Go Ice Skating

If you're feeling adventurous, head to one of NYC's ice skating rinks for some indoor fun. The rink at Rockefeller Center is a classic option, but you can also check out the rinks at Bryant Park or Chelsea Piers. Just make sure to bring warm clothes!

In Conclusion

As you can see, there are plenty of things to do on a rainy day in NYC. Whether you want to stay indoors or venture out into the rain, there's something for everyone. So next time the weather is less than ideal, don't let it get you down. Instead, try one of the activities above and make the most of your day.
