Can You Build Muscle On A 3 Day Split

Written by Jordan Mar 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re someone who’s looking to build muscle, you might be wondering if a 3 day split is enough to help you achieve your goals. In this article, we’ll take a closer look at what a 3 day split is, how it works, and whether or not it’s effective when it comes to building muscle.

What is a 3 Day Split?

A 3 day split is a type of workout routine where you work out different muscle groups on different days of the week. For example, you might work out your chest, triceps, and shoulders on Monday, your back and biceps on Wednesday, and your legs on Friday.

This type of split is popular among those who are looking to build muscle because it allows for more focused and intense workouts on specific muscle groups. It also provides ample time for rest and recovery between workouts.

How Does a 3 Day Split Work?

A 3 day split typically involves working out one or two muscle groups per day, with each workout lasting around 60-90 minutes. The goal is to perform compound exercises that target multiple muscle groups at once, as well as isolation exercises that target specific muscles.

By focusing on one or two muscle groups per day, you can give those muscles the attention they need to grow and develop. You can also vary the intensity and volume of your workouts based on the specific muscle groups you’re targeting.

Can You Build Muscle on a 3 Day Split?

The short answer is yes, you can build muscle on a 3 day split. However, there are a few factors to keep in mind:

1. Your Diet

In order to build muscle, you need to be in a calorie surplus. This means you need to consume more calories than you burn each day. You also need to make sure you’re consuming enough protein to support muscle growth.

2. Your Workouts

Your workouts need to be intense and focused in order to stimulate muscle growth. This means using heavy weights and performing compound exercises that target multiple muscle groups at once.

3. Your Rest and Recovery

Your muscles need time to rest and recover after workouts in order to grow and develop. This means getting enough sleep each night, taking rest days when needed, and avoiding overtraining.

How to Build Muscle on a 3 Day Split

If you’re looking to build muscle on a 3 day split, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Focus on Compound Exercises

Compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, bench press, and overhead press are great for building muscle because they target multiple muscle groups at once. Make sure to include these exercises in your workouts.

2. Vary Your Rep Ranges

Varying your rep ranges can help keep your muscles guessing and prevent plateaus. Aim for 8-12 reps per set for muscle growth, but also include some sets of 4-6 reps for strength and some sets of 12-15 reps for endurance.

3. Get Enough Protein

Aim to consume at least 1 gram of protein per pound of bodyweight each day. This will help support muscle growth and repair.

4. Rest and Recover

Make sure to take rest days when needed, and get enough sleep each night. This will give your muscles time to recover and grow.

Pros and Cons of a 3 Day Split

Pros:

  • Allows for focused and intense workouts on specific muscle groups
  • Provides ample time for rest and recovery between workouts
  • Can be effective for building muscle and strength

Cons:

  • May not provide enough volume for some individuals
  • May not be ideal for those who enjoy full-body workouts
  • May not be as effective for advanced lifters

FAQ

1. Is a 3 day split good for beginners?

Yes, a 3 day split can be a good option for beginners who are looking to build muscle. It allows for focused and intense workouts on specific muscle groups, which can be beneficial for building strength and muscle.

2. Can you do cardio on a 3 day split?

Yes, you can do cardio on a 3 day split. Just make sure to schedule your cardio workouts on days when you’re not working out the same muscle groups.

3. How long should a 3 day split workout last?

A 3 day split workout should last around 60-90 minutes, depending on the intensity and volume of your workouts.

Conclusion

Overall, a 3 day split can be an effective way to build muscle and strength. By focusing on one or two muscle groups per day, you can give those muscles the attention they need to grow and develop. Just make sure to follow the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, and you’ll be on your way to building the muscle you want.
