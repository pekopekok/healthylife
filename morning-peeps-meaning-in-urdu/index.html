What is the Meaning of "Morning Peeps" in Urdu?

"Morning peeps" is a common greeting used in Urdu to say hello to someone in the morning. The word "peeps" is derived from the English language and is a slang term that means "people" or "friends." When combined with "morning," it becomes a friendly way to greet someone and wish them a good day ahead.

How to Use "Morning Peeps" in Urdu

If you want to use "morning peeps" in Urdu, here's how:

  1. Start by saying "subah bakhair" which means "good morning."
  2. Follow it with "peeps" to greet your friends or people you know well.

For example, you can say "subah bakhair peeps" to greet your friends or colleagues in the morning. It's a friendly and informal way to start your day and show your appreciation for the people in your life.
