Welcome to our guide to the ABC Thursday Night Schedule! If you're a fan of the network's lineup, you won't want to miss this breakdown of all the shows you can expect to see on Thursday nights. From drama to comedy, we've got you covered.

What Shows Can You Expect to See?

ABC's Thursday night lineup is always packed with some of the network's most popular shows. Here's a breakdown of what you can expect to see:

  • Grey's Anatomy
  • Station 19
  • A Million Little Things
  • Big Sky

Grey's Anatomy

Grey's Anatomy has been a staple of ABC's Thursday night lineup for years. The show follows the personal and professional lives of the doctors at Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital in Seattle. Tune in to see the latest medical drama and romance unfold.

Station 19

If you're a fan of Grey's Anatomy, you won't want to miss Station 19. This spinoff series follows the lives of the firefighters at Station 19, which is located just three blocks away from Grey Sloan Memorial Hospital. Catch all the action and drama on Thursday nights.

A Million Little Things

A Million Little Things is a drama series that follows a group of friends in Boston who are all struggling with their own personal issues. The show explores themes of friendship, love, loss, and mental health. Tune in on Thursday nights to see how their stories unfold.

Big Sky

Big Sky is a crime drama series that follows a private detective and an ex-cop as they search for two sisters who have been kidnapped by a truck driver in Montana. The show is full of twists and turns, and you won't want to miss a single episode on Thursday nights.

How to Watch the ABC Thursday Night Schedule

If you're looking to catch all your favorite ABC shows on Thursday nights, there are a few ways you can tune in. Here are some of the most popular options:

  • Watch live on ABC: If you have a cable or satellite subscription, you can watch ABC live on Thursday nights.
  • Stream on Hulu: Hulu offers a live TV package that includes ABC, so you can watch the shows live as they air.
  • Stream on ABC.com: If you have a cable or satellite subscription, you can also stream ABC shows on the network's website.

Pros and Cons of Watching the ABC Thursday Night Schedule

Like any TV lineup, there are pros and cons to watching the ABC Thursday Night Schedule. Here are a few to consider:

Pros:

  • Great variety of shows: With drama, comedy, and crime series, there's something for everyone on ABC's Thursday night lineup.
  • Consistent schedule: If you're a creature of habit, you'll appreciate that the shows air at the same time every week.
  • Easy to stream: With options like Hulu and ABC.com, it's easy to watch the shows even if you don't have cable.

Cons:

  • Competing shows: If you're a fan of shows on other networks that air at the same time, you'll have to choose which ones to watch live.
  • Not every show is for everyone: While the variety is great, not everyone will be interested in every show on the lineup.
  • Commercials: If you're watching live or streaming with commercials, you'll have to sit through some ads.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time do the shows air on Thursday nights?

The shows typically air from 8:00pm to 11:00pm Eastern time.

Can I watch the shows if I don't have cable?

Yes, you can watch the shows on Hulu or ABC.com if you don't have cable. Both options require a subscription, however.

When do new episodes of the shows air?

New episodes typically air on Thursday nights, but there may be occasional breaks or special events that interrupt the schedule.

Are there any other ways to watch the shows?

Some episodes may be available for purchase on services like iTunes or Amazon. You can also check with your local library to see if they have the DVDs available for loan.
