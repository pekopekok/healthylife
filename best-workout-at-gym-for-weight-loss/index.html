Are you looking for the best workout at the gym for weight loss? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which exercises to include in your workout routine. In this article, we will discuss the top workouts to help you lose weight effectively and efficiently.

Table of Contents

  • Cardio Workouts
  • Strength Training
  • HIIT Workouts
  • FAQs

Cardio Workouts

Cardio workouts are excellent for weight loss because they help you burn calories and fat. The following cardio exercises are great options:

  • Treadmill Running: Running on a treadmill is a popular cardio exercise that can help you burn up to 600 calories per hour.
  • Jumping Rope: Jumping rope is a fun and effective way to burn calories. You can burn up to 500 calories in just 30 minutes.
  • Elliptical Trainer: Using an elliptical trainer is a low-impact workout that can help you burn up to 400 calories per hour.
  • Rowing Machine: Rowing is a full-body workout that can help you burn up to 800 calories per hour.

Include at least 30 minutes of cardio in your workout routine, at least 3-4 times a week.

Strength Training

Strength training is an essential part of any weight loss journey. Building muscle can help you burn more calories even when you're not working out. The following strength training exercises are excellent for weight loss:

  • Squats: Squats are a great exercise to target your legs, glutes, and core. They can help you burn up to 200 calories in just 15 minutes.
  • Deadlifts: Deadlifts are a compound exercise that targets multiple muscle groups. They can help you burn up to 300 calories in just 20 minutes.
  • Bench Press: Bench press is an excellent exercise to build upper body strength. It can help you burn up to 150 calories in just 15 minutes.
  • Push-ups: Push-ups are a bodyweight exercise that targets your chest, arms, and core. They can help you burn up to 100 calories in just 10 minutes.

Include at least 2-3 strength training exercises in your workout routine, at least 2-3 times a week.

HIIT Workouts

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) workouts are a combination of cardio and strength training exercises that can help you burn more calories in less time. The following HIIT exercises are great options:

  • Burpees: Burpees are a full-body exercise that can help you burn up to 15 calories per minute.
  • Mountain Climbers: Mountain climbers are a cardio exercise that can help you burn up to 10 calories per minute.
  • Kettlebell Swings: Kettlebell swings are a strength training exercise that can help you burn up to 12 calories per minute.
  • Jump Squats: Jump squats are a plyometric exercise that can help you burn up to 12 calories per minute.

Include at least 20-30 minutes of HIIT workouts in your workout routine, at least 2-3 times a week.

FAQs

What is the best time to exercise for weight loss?

The best time to exercise for weight loss is in the morning on an empty stomach. This is because your body has fasted overnight, and your glycogen stores are low. As a result, your body will burn more fat for energy during your workout.

How long should I exercise for weight loss?

To see significant weight loss results, you should aim to exercise for at least 30-60 minutes per session, at least 3-4 times a week. However, you can still see benefits from shorter workouts, such as 20-30 minutes of HIIT or cardio.

Can I lose weight without exercise?

While it's possible to lose weight without exercise, it's not recommended. Exercise helps you burn more calories, build muscle, and improve your overall health. Combining exercise with a healthy diet is the best way to achieve sustainable weight loss.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the best workout at the gym for weight loss includes a combination of cardio, strength training, and HIIT workouts. Incorporating these exercises into your workout routine, along with a healthy diet, can help you achieve your weight loss goals effectively and efficiently. Remember to consult with a professional trainer before starting any new exercise routine to ensure you're performing exercises correctly and safely.
