Frequently Asked Questions

What is the significance of Republic Day in India?

Republic Day is celebrated on January 26 to mark the date on which the Constitution of India came into effect in 1950. It is a national holiday and is celebrated with parades and cultural programs across the country.

Why is Independence Day important in India?

Independence Day is celebrated on August 15 to commemorate the country's independence from British rule in 1947. It is a national holiday and is celebrated with flag hoisting ceremonies and cultural programs.
