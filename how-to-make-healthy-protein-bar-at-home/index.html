How To Make Healthy Protein Bar At Home

Written by Bowie May 01, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of buying expensive protein bars from the store that are loaded with sugar and other unhealthy ingredients? Well, you're in luck because today we're going to show you how to make your own healthy protein bars at home! Not only will they save you money, but they're also customizable to your taste and dietary needs. Let's get started with the ingredients you'll need:

Ingredients:

- 2 cups of rolled oats
- 1 cup of almond flour
- ½ cup of protein powder
- ½ cup of almond butter
- ½ cup of honey
- 1 tsp. of vanilla extract
- 1 tsp. of cinnamon
- ½ cup of chopped nuts or dried fruits (optional)

How to:

Step 1: Preheat the Oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2: Mix the Dry Ingredients

In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, almond flour, protein powder, and cinnamon. Mix well.

Step 3: Mix the Wet Ingredients

In a separate mixing bowl, combine the almond butter, honey, and vanilla extract. Mix well until smooth.

Step 4: Combine the Ingredients

Pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture and mix well until everything is combined. If you're adding nuts or dried fruits, now is the time to add them in and mix well.

Step 5: Press the Mixture into a Baking Dish

Transfer the mixture into a baking dish and press it down evenly with your hands or a spatula. Make sure it's tightly packed.

Step 6: Bake the Protein Bars

Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.

Step 7: Cut and Serve

Let the protein bars cool for at least 10 minutes before cutting them into bars. Serve and enjoy!

Tips:

- Store the protein bars in an airtight container in the fridge for up to a week.
- You can use any type of nut butter or sweetener that you prefer.
- Customize the recipe by adding in your favorite nuts, seeds, or dried fruits!

Solution:

Making your own healthy protein bars at home is a great solution for those who are looking for a quick and easy snack that's also good for you. Plus, you can customize them to your taste and dietary needs!

FAQ:

Q. Can I use a different type of flour?
A. Yes, you can use any type of flour that you prefer. However, almond flour is a great gluten-free option.

Q. Can I use a different type of protein powder?
A. Yes, you can use any type of protein powder that you prefer. Just make sure it's a high-quality, clean protein powder.

Q. Can I add in other ingredients?
A. Yes, you can customize the recipe by adding in your favorite nuts, seeds, or dried fruits!

Pros and Cons:

Pros:

- Customizable to your taste and dietary needs
- Cheaper than buying store-bought protein bars
- Healthier than store-bought protein bars

Cons:

- Takes a little bit of time to make
- May not be as convenient as store-bought protein bars
